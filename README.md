Overview of the Project 
Project Name : SOCIAL MEDIA STRATEGY PLAN

Target Audience :
1. Aspirational Youth
Students & young professionals. ~75% of revenue. Prioritise aesthetics, bass-heavy audio & social validation.
2. Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities
50%+ of new customer acquisitions. Driven by rising 5G penetration. View boAt as an accessible lifestyle symbol.
3. Psychographic Profile
IPL fans, BGMI gamers, OTT binge-watchers. Want stylish tech without Apple/Sony pricing.

360° Marketing Architecture :
1. Online (Digital)
High-frequency social posts, meme marketing, aggressive targeting on Amazon, Flipkart & Instagram.
2. Experiential
Sponsors youth festivals — Sunburn, Lollapalooza India, college fests — with physical product experience zones.
3.Offline (Mass Media)
TV & digital billboard campaigns during IPL and high-visibility national events.

Key Marketing Strategies:
1. Influencer Marketing
Tier 1 celebs (Hardik Pandya, Kiara Advani) + thousands of micro-influencers showing real-life product use.
2. Lifestyle Positioning
Sells feelings over specs. Products marketed like fashion accessories with trendy colorways — not gadgets.
3. Meme Marketing
Moment marketing on viral trends, Shark Tank references, and relatable Gen-Z humour to feel like a peer, not a brand.
4. Hashtag & UGC Strategy
#boAtheads drives organic community. #DoWhatBoatsYourFloat builds a brand philosophy around youth aspiration.

Platform Focus :
1. Instagram — Primary
Lifestyle reels, product launches, influencer collabs, and instant traffic generation.
2. LinkedIn — Secondary
Corporate milestones, Make in India initiatives, founder Aman Gupta's business insights.
